Lasagne

Description

Enjoy a classic family meal with this recipe featuring a blend of smooth ricotta, melt-in-your-mouth mozzarella, and rich Parmesan. Here's to a flavourful evening that everyone will love. Perfect for bringing the family together over a delicious, cheese-infused meal!

Ingredients

  • 500g Quality Mark beef mince
  • 250g lasagne sheets
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion peeled and chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves crushed
  • 325g Delmaine Pomadoro sauce
  • 250ml red wine or beef stock
  • 1 Tbsp Delmaine red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • cheese sauce
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 250g sour cream
  • 1 egg lightly beaten
  • 75g grated Parmesan cheese
  • topping
  • 125g grated Mozzarella cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese grated

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 190Â°C. Place oil in a large saucepan on medium heat and cook onions until they are soft and glassy. Add garlic and cook until soft.
  2. Add mince and stir until fully browned. Mix in Pomodoro sauce, vinegar, wine, and seasonings. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Prepare cheese sauce by combining all ingredients.
  4. Grease a rectangular 2.5 litre baking dish and line with a layer of lasagne. Spread with half the meat sauce and dot with a quarter of the cheese sauce. Repeat the same layer structure again.
  5. Top with another layer of lasagne. Spread top with remaining cheese sauce and sprinkle with Mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake for 35 - 40 minutes. Stand for 5 minutes and then cut into squares to serve.
